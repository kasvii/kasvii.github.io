Yingxuan You (游盈萱)

I'm a third-year master student in the School of Computer Science, Peking University (PKU), China, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Liu. Before joining PKU, I obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Automation Science from Beihang University in June 2021.

My research interest is human-centric visual understanding, focusing on 3D human pose estimation / mesh reconstruction / texture generation.
